chemical equation

is the symbolic representation of a chemical reaction in the form of symbols and chemical formulas.A chemical equation onsists of a list of reactants (the starting substances) on the left-hand side,an arrow symbol,and a list of products (substances formed in the chemical reaction) on the right-hand side. 
Chemical equations were first formulated by the 
 chemist Jean Beguin in the year 1615.
